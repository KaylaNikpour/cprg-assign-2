About Me!

I am a student enrolled in an Interactive Design program at SAIT (Southern Alberta Institute of Technology). Over my first year of school, I learned how to use Adobe applications (Photoshop, Illustrator, In Design, Premiere Pro, After Effects, Audition), as well as tackled a version control system in tune with VSCode. I have learned the essential of User Experience and continue to grow my knowledge in that field. I have discovered I really enjoy coding and find it fascinating. I also really love design. I think it is so cool having the ability to create anything I want. The most exciting thing I learned this year is how to properly use Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Audition. I love anything to do with film so having these new set of skills is so beneficial for me. In addition to school, I love reading and going on walks. I also stream on twitch at least once a week where I practice my coding and play games for my followers to see.
